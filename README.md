# AI Investment Trends Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes funding trends among leading artificial intelligence companies between 2014 and 2025 using Python and Matplotlib.

The objective is to identify major investment patterns, compare funding among companies, and examine the relationship between funding and company valuation.

## Dataset

Dataset: AI Startup Funding Database (2014–2025)

The dataset contains information about:

- Company names
- Funding rounds
- Funding amounts
- Company valuations
- Industry sectors
- Geographic locations
- Investment dates

## Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab
- GitHub

## Visualizations

### 1. Top 10 AI Companies by Cumulative Funding Raised

Ranks companies by total funding received.

### 2. Total AI Funding by Year

Shows how AI investment has changed over time.

### 3. Funding Raised vs. Post-Money Valuation

Examines the relationship between funding and company valuation.

## Key Findings

- OpenAI received the highest cumulative funding in the dataset.
- AI investment increased significantly after 2022.
- Higher funding levels generally correspond to higher company valuations.
- Investor interest in AI has accelerated rapidly in recent years.
